# Product Brief

Product: Fluxa

Audience:
- Small software teams with 5 to 40 people
- PMs and engineering leads who feel buried in status updates

Positioning:
- Fluxa turns scattered work updates into one clear weekly snapshot
- Emphasize clarity, calm, and momentum

Required sections:
- Hero
- Proof
- Features
- Pricing
- FAQ
- CTA

Copy rules:
- Use the product name Fluxa
- Include one concise headline and one supporting subhead in the hero
- Mention a weekly snapshot, fewer status meetings, and clearer ownership
- Do not claim AI autonomy, guaranteed revenue, or enterprise certifications

Visual rules:
- The page should feel credible and modern, not playful
- Avoid default browser styling
- Include responsive behavior for narrow screens
